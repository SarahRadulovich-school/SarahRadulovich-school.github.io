Are you eligible to Vote?

To register and be able to vote in the federal Canadian Elections, you must be a Canadian Citizen aged 18 or older, you must be able to provide acceptable proof of identity, which includes an address. Although this year is different, citizens are asked to checkmark a box proving they are over the age of 18 and documentation can be provided at a different date.
