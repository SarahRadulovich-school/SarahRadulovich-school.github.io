Instructions on How To Vote

If you are participating in this years elections either in the polling place or online, below are posted instructions on how to complete the polls appropriately. Please make sure to read each step carefully and follow these instructions to the best of your ability. The identity and address proof for online voters has been changed this year, in prior years you needed to provide the information right before the election, now after you have casted your vote you will be sent a email in which you will need to provide the information, if not your ballot will not be pushed through and successfully completed, hence meaning your vote won't count.

Voting In a Polling Place Instructions -

  • Step 1- When you enter the polling place, an election worker greets you and shows you to the right table.
  • Step 2- At your table, show your proof of identity and address.
  • Step 3- The election worker will initial, fold and hand you a ballot.
  • Step 4- Go behind the voting screen, mark and refold your ballot to keep it secret.
  • Step 5- Return your ballot to the worker so they can tear off the tab.
  • Step 6- Put your ballot in the box.

Voting Online Instructions -

  • Step 1- When the page loads assess the information.
  • Step 2- Fill out each field with your decisions.
  • Step 3- Review your answers, make sure each field is completed.
  • Step 4- Press "Submit Ballot" and let the page finish loading.
  • Step 5- Provide your proof of identity and address, when emailed and asked.
